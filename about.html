I'm Rutuja

Hi, I’m Rutuja Deorey and here is a little about me. At 16, I went on a year long student exchange in Germany through Rotary. I stayed with four different German families and travelled all around Europe with other exchange students.

I came back home with memories that still make me laugh the same and friends all around the world, but I also brought back 15 kilos of extra weight on me. I did not regret it, not once. But for the sake of good health, it was time to lose the extra weight.

The one thing I was sure of was not subjecting my body to any fast fat burning ‘diets’. I wanted to take my time and learn what it is to be healthy. It took 9 months of experimenting, researching and learning, dealing with frustration on some days. I was finally there-it wasn’t a number on the weighing scale. It was a feeling of vitality and strength, of respecting my body and truly learning to listen to it. ‘Diet’, to me is not a short term food schedule. It’s a healthy way of life, to make sure I am fit enough to capitalise my potential. Good health is an investment that pays you back everyday!

I had always been curious about modelling and decided to go for it, now that I was feeling good about myself. I sent my portfolio to the best agencies in town but faced rejection every six months. Finally, in my third year of engineering college, I signed a contract with one.

My modelling experience so far, exposure to different brands and what they stand for makes me question the footprint of our choices. Since, I was a kid I have been deeply passionate about the environment. Now that I think of it, caring for the planet is not just a one day plantation drive, but every single choice we make to pick products and services in our day to day life.

I made this blog to share everything I have learnt so far and keep exploring. It is our responsibility to make mindful choices for both our personal and our environmental well being. My blog is to help make your job easier. You’ll see how it's the most simple things we have access to that make all the difference!
