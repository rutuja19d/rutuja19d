Drink Green Tea for weight loss

Firstly, please remember that no one particular thing results in ‘weight loss’. Drinking green tea but eating unhealthy food at irregular times and not exercising will not help you lose weight ever. This article is about using green tea to its potential to maximise your efforts in your weight loss journey. It creates a positive impact on an all-round balanced lifestyle. Irrespective of weight loss, green tea is definitely a healthy drink to consume in the day.

AVOID DRINKING GREEN TEA ON EMPTY STOMACH IN THE MORNING.

When should you drink Green Tea?

Have your cup of green tea: 2 HOURS BEFORE/AFTER A MEAL
So, if you have your breakfast at 9 am, have a cup of green tea at 11, or after lunch at 4 pm.
Do not have more than 2 cups of green tea a day.

How does Green Tea help?

Green tea is loaded with antioxidants. It contains a type of flavonoid called catechin, which can help break down excess fat.

It also boosts metabolism allowing you to use your energy well throughout the day.

To burn fat, your body must first break it down in the fat cell and move it into your bloodstream. The active compounds in green tea can aid this process by boosting the effects of some fat-burning hormones, such as norepinephrine. The main antioxidant in tea, EGCG (Epigallocatechin gallate), can help inhibit an enzyme that breaks down the hormone norepinephrine. When this enzyme is inhibited, the amount of norepinephrine increases, promoting fat breakdown. Several studies suggest that taking green tea extract or EGCG supplements can make you burn more calories — even at rest. In most studies, this amounts to about a 3–4% increase, some show an increase as high as 8%. For a person who burns 2,000 calories per day, 3–4% amounts to an additional 60–80 calories spent per day.

Which kind of Green Tea is good for you?

Now, along with when should you drink green tea, the other thing to keep in mind is which kind of green tea to be consumed.

Green Tea leaves win over green tea sachets anyday. In addition to leaves, you want to choose ‘Matcha Green Tea’, they are known to be the richest source of antioxidants and nutrients.

How to brew your tea?

When you brew your tea, do not add boiling hot water to tea. Let it rest for about 5-7 mins and cool. Adding boiling water is bad for the catechin.
