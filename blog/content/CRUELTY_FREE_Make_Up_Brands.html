It’s going to be a long journey towards vegan cosmetics and the ball is in the court of customers, we need to ask for what’s necessary to safeguard those directly affected by this vast industry.
We can least start with using cruelty free brands.

What is Cruelty Free make up?
Cruelty-free cosmetics is a category containing all cosmetics that have not been tested on animals.
 
India doesn’t permit for animal testing but major international brands available here (that we use daily) still do animal testing elsewhere. They are therefore, NOT cruelty free.
You can check the packaging to see if the product has a cruelty free logo.
There are some companies whose parent company is not a cruelty free brand, there has always been a debate of whether these brands are cruelty free or not in that case.
