20 FAT LOSS FOODS IN EVERY INDIAN KITCHEN

Many people ask about what to eat in the second half of the day, other than our basic 3 'main' meals that we have known. It is quite necessary that you include any of these throughout your day in your lunch or dinner and/or pre/post it if you are on your journey to a fat loss or rather just a healthy lifestyle.

Just grab the easy ones to carry if you are traveling for work/college!


  1. Moong Daal
    -- Vitamin A,B,C,E, potassium, calcium, iron 
    -- Not necessary to have it during lunch or dinner.
    Mix it with spinach to make a soup or soak overnight and mix with veggies to have as sprouts
  2. Walnuts
    -- Better than all other nuts
    -- Brain health, antioxidants, vitamins, omega 3 fatty acid, manganese, copper, protein, fibre.
  3. Spinach
    -- Very low in calories, vitamin A,K, calcium, iron, fibre, protein, zinc
    -- Keeps digestive tract on track
  4. Bitter gourd (karela)
    -- Maintains blood sugar level in your body, 90% water so great for weight loss
  5. Beetroot
    -- Boosts stamina, low in calories, rich in nutrients
  6. Almonds
    -- Keeps sugar level in check, great stimulant so helps in weight loss
  7. Apple
      -- Highest fibre, boosts metabolism and helps fat loss
  8. Beans
    -- Fibre and protein, great for cardiovascular system, maintains bone health, helps in weight loss, healthy digestion
  9. Cauliflower
    - - Very high fibre
  10. Cinnamon
    -- Half teaspoon post meal prevents spike in insulin
  11. Turmeric
    -- Keeps fatty tissue at minimum
  12. Radish
    -- Again high fibre food
  13. Garlic
      -- Fights high BP, cholesterol, blood sugar level
  14. Lentils
    -- Protein and fibre
  15. Bananas
    -- BEST for pre or post workout to reduce bad fat
  16. Tomatoes
    -- Low in calories, acts as a detoxifier
  17. Olive Oil
    -- Prevent any chance of diabetes ever, improves overall health like nothing other
  18. Cabbage
    -- VERY low in calories, best weight loss snack high in fibre, fights ageing
  19. Eggs
    -- Powerhouse of protein
  20. Coffee
    -- Black coffee before workout boosts metabolism
