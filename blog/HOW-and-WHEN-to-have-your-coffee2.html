HOW & WHEN to have your coffee without it badly affecting you?

Consumption of coffee specially in young adults has increased drastically in India.
Coffee is : high in antioxidants, linked to reduced risk of many diseases, increases brain activity, boosts metabolism. However these effects are short term. When you drink coffee everyday, you build a tolerance to it and effects can be less powerful. Ill effects include addiction, tiredness, irritability, increade in cholestrol levels, early aging.

So, The most basic rule : DO NOT add anything unhealthy to your coffee like artificial creamer. Sugar is also considered to be bad when consumed with coffee. But in light of practicality a little bit of sugar wouldn't be the end of the world.


  1. How to have coffee?
    Brew your coffee. (If you don’t have paper filters, buy them asap. Readily available.)
    Unfiltered coffee contains cafestol which increase cholesterol levels to another extent.
  2. WHEN is the best time to have coffee and WHY?
    This is the most important part. NEVER have it as the first thing in the morning. Cortisol is released three times a day. Most prominently in the morning. It’s a hormone released in response to stress & low blood glucose. When consumed at times cortisol is released, the body produces less of it and relies on caffeine to compensate. Release generally : 8-9 am, 12-1 pm, 5-6pm So have your coffee accordingly between 10 and noon or 2 to 4.
  3. How many cups of coffee?
    Typically 3-4 cups of coffee is considered to be safe. But try to not have more than 2.
  4. Coffee for weight loss
    Black coffee without sugar is an effective pre workout drink. Consuming black coffee before workout aids in burning more fat during the workout session. It also helps in enhancing metabolism which thus allowing you to burn more fat throughout the day.
